## Required Assets

### App Icon
- **Size**: 1024x1024 pixels
- **Format**: PNG (no transparency)
- **Location**: `assets/icons/app-icon-1024x1024.png`
- **Requirements**: 
  - No rounded corners (Apple adds them automatically)
  - No alpha channel
  - High quality, professional design

### Screenshots

#### iPhone 6.7" Display (iPhone 15 Pro Max, etc.)
- **Size**: 1290x2796 pixels
- **Required**: At least 3 screenshots
- **Location**: `assets/screenshots/iphone-67/`

#### iPhone 6.5" Display (iPhone 11 Pro Max, etc.)
- **Size**: 1284x2778 pixels
- **Required**: At least 3 screenshots
- **Location**: `assets/screenshots/iphone-65/`

#### iPhone 5.5" Display (iPhone 8 Plus, etc.)
- **Size**: 1242x2208 pixels
- **Required**: At least 3 screenshots
- **Location**: `assets/screenshots/iphone-55/`

#### iPad Pro 12.9" Display
- **Size**: 2048x2732 pixels
- **Required**: At least 3 screenshots
- **Location**: `assets/screenshots/ipad-pro-129/`

### Screenshot Guidelines
- Show key features and user flows
- Demonstrate card creation process
- Highlight AI-generated sayings
- Show face detection in action
- Display premium features
- Use real content (not placeholders)
- Professional, polished appearance
- No device frames needed (Apple adds them)

### Marketing Image (Optional)
- **Size**: Various sizes supported
- **Location**: `assets/marketing/`
- Used for App Store marketing campaigns
