### Usage Instructions

**Creating Your First Card:**

1. **Import Photos**: Tap the photo button to import your face and your loved one's face
2. **Choose Template**: Browse templates and select one that speaks to you
3. **Generate Sayings**: Tap "Generate Sayings" and provide a brief inspiration (optional)
4. **Customize**: Add text, images, stickers, or adjust layout
5. **Preview**: See how your card looks before sharing
6. **Share**: Tap share to send to social media or save to Photos

**Tips for Best Results:**
- Use clear, well-lit photos for best face detection
- Try different templates to find your style
- Experiment with AI sayings - regenerate for more options
- Add stickers or images for extra personality
