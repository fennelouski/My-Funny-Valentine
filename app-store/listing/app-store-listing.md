Create the perfect Valentine's Day card with My Funny Valentine! Our app combines AI-powered creativity with your personal photos to help you express your feelings in a unique and heartfelt way.

### Key Features

**🤖 AI-Powered Sayings**
Generate personalized Valentine's messages with our intelligent AI assistant. Just provide a little inspiration, and get 10 unique sayings tailored to your relationship. Perfect for when you need that extra spark of creativity!

**📸 Face Detection & Integration**
Import photos of yourself and your loved one. Our advanced face detection automatically places faces perfectly on card templates, creating professional-looking cards in seconds.

**🎨 Multiple Image Sources**
- Use Apple Image Playground to generate custom romantic images
- Import iPhone stickers for fun decorations
- Use Smart Cutout to extract faces from any photo
- Add your own photos and images

**💝 Beautiful Card Templates**
Choose from a variety of professionally designed templates. Each template is optimized for face placement and text, ensuring your card looks amazing.

**☁️ iCloud Sync**
All your cards sync automatically across all your Apple devices. Start creating on your iPhone, finish on your iPad, and share from anywhere.

**📱 Easy Sharing**
Share your creations directly to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or save to your Photos. Export animated GIFs on macOS for even more sharing options.

- 20 AI-generated sayings per month (vs 3 free)

- Early access to new templates
- Priority support
