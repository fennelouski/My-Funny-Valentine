# App Icon Directory

## Required Asset

**File**: `app-icon-1024x1024.png`

## Specifications

- **Size**: 1024x1024 pixels
- **Format**: PNG
- **Color Space**: RGB
- **Transparency**: Not allowed (no alpha channel)
- **Rounded Corners**: Not needed (Apple adds them automatically)

## Design Guidelines

- High quality, professional design
- Represents your app clearly
- Works well at small sizes
- Follows Apple's design guidelines
- No text (unless it's part of the icon design)
- No screenshots or UI elements

## Tips

- Test your icon at various sizes
- Ensure it's recognizable at 60x60 (home screen size)
- Use high contrast for visibility
- Avoid fine details that won't show at small sizes
- Consider dark mode appearance
