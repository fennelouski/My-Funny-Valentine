# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 12, 2026**

## Introduction

My Funny Valentine ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Photos**: When you import photos to create cards, these are stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud account
- **Text Input**: Any text you enter, including custom card text and AI inspiration prompts
- **Account Information**: Your Apple ID information (managed by Apple, not directly by us)

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the App (features used, cards created)
- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version (for compatibility)
- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active premium subscription (managed through App Store)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identification information beyond what Apple provides
- We do not access your contacts, location, or other personal data
- We do not track you across other apps or websites
- We do not sell your data to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Services

- **Card Creation**: Process your photos and text to create personalized cards
- **AI Generation**: Send your inspiration prompts to our AI service to generate sayings
- **Cloud Sync**: Sync your cards across your Apple devices via CloudKit
- **Subscription Management**: Verify and manage your premium subscription status

### Data Processing

- **Face Detection**: Performed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework
- **AI Requests**: Your inspiration prompts are sent to our backend API (hosted on Vercel) to generate sayings
- **Image Generation**: If you use premium image generation, descriptions are sent to our backend API

### Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your cards and photos are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **iCloud Storage**: Cards sync to your private CloudKit database (encrypted end-to-end)
- **Backend Caching**: AI responses may be cached temporarily to improve performance and reduce costs

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### CloudKit (iCloud)

- Your cards and photos sync to your private CloudKit container
- Data is encrypted end-to-end by Apple
- We cannot access your CloudKit data
- Only you can access your data through your Apple ID

### Third-Party Services

- **OpenAI API**: When you request AI-generated sayings or images, your prompts are sent to OpenAI's API
  - We use cost-optimized models (GPT-OSS-20b or GPT-5 Nano)
  - Prompts are sent securely over HTTPS
  - OpenAI's privacy policy applies to their processing
  - We do not share your photos with OpenAI

- **Vercel**: Our backend API is hosted on Vercel
  - Vercel's privacy policy applies
  - Data is processed in accordance with their terms

### We Do NOT Share

- We do not sell your data
- We do not share your photos with third parties (except as necessary for AI text generation)
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not share your data with data brokers

## Data Security

### Security Measures

- **End-to-End Encryption**: CloudKit data is encrypted by Apple
- **HTTPS**: All API communications use secure HTTPS
- **On-Device Processing**: Face detection happens entirely on your device
- **Minimal Data Collection**: We only collect data necessary for app functionality

### Your Responsibility

- Keep your Apple ID secure
- Do not share your device with unauthorized users
- Use strong device passcodes

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Deletion

- **View Your Data**: All your cards are accessible in the App
- **Delete Cards**: Delete any card at any time from within the App
- **Delete All Data**: Uninstalling the App removes local data; CloudKit data can be deleted from iCloud settings
- **Export Data**: You can export cards by sharing them or saving to Photos

### Subscription Management

- Manage your subscription in App Store settings
- Cancel anytime without losing access until the end of your billing period
- Restore purchases on new devices

### AI Request Limits

- Free users: 3 AI requests per day
- Premium users: 20 AI requests per month
- Limits reset automatically (daily for free, monthly for premium)

## Children's Privacy

- Our App is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately

## International Users

- By using the App, you consent to this transfer
